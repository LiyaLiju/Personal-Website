My Creative Hobbies

Crochet

Crochet is one of my favorite ways to unwind. I started learning during the pandemic when I was looking for a calming activity to pass the time at home. What began as a simple attempt to make a hat has turned into a full-blown passion. I love how portable crochet is and that I can bring my yarn and hook anywhere and work on projects while watching TV, waiting for appointments, or even during long car rides. The repetitive motions of crocheting are incredibly meditative, and I find that it helps me manage stress and decompress.

Over the years, I have made scarves, hats, amigurumi stuffed animals, bags, and even clothing items. Each project teaches me something new, whether it is a new stitch pattern or how to read more complex patterns. I especially love choosing color combinations and seeing how different yarns work up into finished pieces. Gifting a handmade item to someone you care about brings a special joy.
